FAQ

1. What is KIOM?

Kenos International Outreach Mission is the missionary branch of Kenos Tabernacle focusing on bringing the gospel to underprivileged parts of Africa and the world as per the Leading of the Lord and Commission in Matthew 18: 19.

2. What is our mission?:

To spread the living word to the less fortunate by reaching remote areas in the Southern Africa region and other parts of the world. Our activities are based on preaching the full gospel of the biblical message of this hour offering prayers, teaching, and spiritual support to the needy in the Name of the Savior and Redeemer Jesus – Christ.

3.What is our vision?

The Word of God to reach anyone, everywhere possible giving each soul regardless of his status the opportunity to hear and experience the grace, mercy, and love of God.
